About Us

We didn't start this to be a franchise. We started this because we couldn't find a decent, honest cup of chai in a city full of automated coffee machines. Chai Sutta is an homage to the street corners where friendships are forged, ideas are born, and heartbreak is cured over a steaming kulhad.

Come as you are. Pull up a plastic chair. Light up, sip loud, and soak in the raw vibe.
